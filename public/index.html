About Us

What We Stand For.

At Comfort Cradle Care, we believe that every newborn deserves exceptional care and attention, and every parent deserves peace and tranquillity during this special time.
Our compassionate and highly skilled night nurses are dedicated to providing the highest quality care for your baby, with personalized attention and support that meets your family's unique needs.
From sleep conditioning and training to consulting and training services for your own staff, we are here to help you bring peace and comfort to your newborn's world.

Why Choose Us

Night Nurse Placement

Comfort Cradle provides overnight care and comfort for your loved one by trained night nurses. They monitor health, provide medication and assistance, offer emotional support and communicate with you and the daytime care team. Comfort Cradle gives you peace of mind at night.

Sleep Conditioning and Training

Sleep conditioning and training is a way to improve your sleep and health. It teaches you how to sleep better by changing your habits, environment, and mindset. It also gives you a personalized plan to follow for better sleep hygiene, schedule, and routines. By doing sleep conditioning and training, you can boost your physical and mental well-being, productivity, and performance.

Sleep Training Consulting & Training Services

We offer consulting services to teach parents techniques to help their baby sleep through the night, bringing peace and tranquility to your home. Additionally, we provide comprehensive training in sleep training and conditioning for newborns, ensuring your staff can provide the same level of care and comfort to your baby.
